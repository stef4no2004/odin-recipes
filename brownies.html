A simple brownie recipe using only one pan

Ingredients

  • 400g Dark chocolate
  • 270g Butter
  • 400g Light brown sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • A shot of espresso
  • 190g Plain flour
  • 50g Cocoa powder
  • Optional: 150g White chocolate

Method

  1. In a pan melt the butter with the dark chocolate
  2. Now add the sugar
  3. The turn off the heat and let the dough cool a bit to add the eggs
  4. Add the salt and coffe
  5. Then add the flour and cocoa powder
  6. Now if you want add the white chocolate
  7. Finally bake in 360Â°F for around 40 minutes
  8. Let the brownies cool before you slice them
